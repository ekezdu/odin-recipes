TOMATO SOUP AND GRILLED CHEESE

Description

A classic and timeless recipe, for lunch or dinner, and great when you're sick. This tomato soup with garlic and ginger is sure to boost your spirit when you have a cold, or want to warm up on a cold winter day.

EQUIPMENT

  • Frying pan
  • Emulsifier, blender, or similar is preferred to make the soup smooth and creamy
  • Knives
  • Spatula
  • Medium size pot for the soup

INGREDIENTS

For the Tomato Soup

  • 1 large can of tomato sauce (28 oz/800 g)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium yellow onion, minced
  • 1 tablespoon garlic ginger paste, to taste
  • ¼ cup flour
  • 2 cups chicken broth, or equivalent in chicken bouillon
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ½ cup milk

For the grilled cheese

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 tablespoons of butter, softened
  • 2 slices of your preferred sliced cheese

INSTRUCTIONS

For the tomato soup

  1. In the pot, heat olive oil and melt butter over medium heat.
  2. Once butter is melted, add minced onion, garlic ginger paste, and sugar to pot and cook until onions are clear.
  3. Add flour, and stir until combined.
  4. Add tomato sauce and chicken broth and heat on medium high heat until boiling.
  5. Lower heat, and add milk.
  6. Taste, and add more garlic ginger paste, salt, or pepper to your preferences.
  7. Let simmer for 10 minutes, or until thickened. Add more flour to desired thickness, waiting 5-10 minutes in between.
  8. Blend the soup with an emulsifier, or pour into a blender until smooth.
  9. Set aside, covered, on low heat, to make the grilled cheese.

For the grilled cheese

  1. Heat pan on medium heat.
  2. Spread half the butter on one side of the bread slices.
  3. When the pan is hot, place one slice of bread butter side down. Add slices of cheese on top, and add the second slice of bread, butter side up.
  4. Let cook for about 3 minutes. Using the spatula, peek under the sandwich to check if the bread is toasted to your liking. Continue this until it is.
  5. Using the spatula, flip the sandwich and do the same as above.
